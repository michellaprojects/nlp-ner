Why does this matter?

A single job vacancy can attract 250–750 applicants, yet manual resume screening averages just 7.4 seconds per document — making the process both error-prone and biased. Around 82 % of companies now use AI-based screening tools, and NLP-driven parsing can cut selection cost and duration by up to 85 %. By focusing on skills rather than demographics, fair AI screening helps reduce age, gender, and ethnicity bias. scanNER investigates statistical, neural, and LLM-based NER models to extract key candidate attributes and enhance efficiency and fairness.
